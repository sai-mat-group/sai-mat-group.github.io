Sai Gautam wins the Prof. Priti Shankar Teaching Award

15 Mar 2024

Dr. Sai Gautam Gopalakrishnan was awarded the Prof. Priti Shankar Teaching Award for Assistant Professors by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc). He was conferred the award at the IISc Court meeting on 15th March 2024, which was presided over by the President of the IISc Court, Mr. N. Chandrasekaran, Chairman of Tata Sons.
