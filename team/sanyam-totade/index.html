I was a Masters in Technology (M. Tech.) student at the Materials Engineering Department, Indian Institute of Science, from 2020-22. My research interests include application of computational material science for the prediction of materials behavior. Moreover, I would like to work with materials which can serve as the potential source of renewable and clean form of energy in future.

I completed my Bachelors in Technology in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, where I worked with Prof. Manjusha Thawre for my final year thesis.

Apart from research, I love to play the tabla and the piano, trekking and cooking.

Upon graduating from Indian Institute of Science, I began working with Tata Steel, in their computational sciences group, starting August 2022.

I was involved with the following publication during my tenure with the group.
